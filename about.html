This is My Favorite School

There are several reasons why I think this school is great. This is completely my personal point of view.

The Core Reasons

Freedom

The school gives us more freedom than many other schools. We can do our individual work, follow our interests, and express our opinions freely. Teachers are also very friendly and supportive.

Students

It is true that some students may not be as strong academically as other schools, but when it comes to teamwork, responsibility, and leadership, this school stands out. Seniors help juniors learn and give them opportunities to grow and take responsibility.

Events

When it comes to events, festivals, or sports, both students and teachers perform with great enthusiasm. The way events are organized here is truly impressive.
